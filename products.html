Raspberries

Did you know Serbia is a world leader in raspberry production? We cultivate the prized Ariljska variety, famous for its sweetness, intense aroma, and vibrant red color. Imagine the burst of summer on your tongue with every bite! These delicate berries are not only delicious but also packed with antioxidants, making them a healthy and delightful treat.

Plums

Another Serbian specialty, plums are a cornerstone of our orchards. We offer a variety of plum types, including those perfect for eating fresh or ideal for drying into prunes - a delicious and healthy snack. Look out for the Stanley and Čačanska Lepotica varieties, known for their juicy flesh and sweet flavor. During the fall season, don't miss the opportunity to experience the vibrant Serbian tradition of "Sljivada" - gathering to pick and dry plums for the winter.

Corn

A golden symbol of Serbian agriculture, corn is more than just a side dish. We source corn from our network of family farms, ensuring plump kernels bursting with flavor, perfect for polenta, grilling, or enjoying on the cob. Serbian corn is a versatile ingredient, used in everything from soups and stews to traditional dishes like "Proja," a cornbread staple.

Wheat

The foundation of Serbian bread, wheat is a testament to the rich heritage of our land. Our wheat is grown using sustainable practices, resulting in high-quality flour for fluffy loaves and delectable pastries. Look out for our traditional sourdough loaves, a testament to the time-honored baking techniques passed down through generations.

Sunflowers

These cheerful flowers grace the Serbian countryside and contribute significantly to our agricultural bounty. The sunflower seeds we source are pressed into pure, healthy sunflower oil, perfect for adding a touch of sunshine to your cooking. Serbian sunflower oil is known for its light, nutty flavor, making it a healthy alternative for all your cooking needs.

Cherries

A delightful jewel-toned fruit, sour cherries add a tangy twist to Serbian cuisine. We source these beauties from across the country, ensuring a burst of flavor in every bite. Sour cherries are a key ingredient in jams, juices, and the beloved "Višnjevka" liqueur, a sweet and tart after-dinner treat.

Apples

A widely grown fruit, apples are enjoyed fresh, used for baking, and in cider production. We offer a variety of apple types, from the sweet and juicy Granny Smith to the tart and tangy Granny Smith. Serbian apples are perfect for enjoying on their own, adding a touch of sweetness to salads, or using in traditional desserts like "Palačinke" (pancakes) with apple filling.

Peppers

Grown in various colors and spice levels, peppers are a popular addition to Serbian cuisine. We offer a range of peppers, from the mild and sweet to the fiery hot. Whether you crave the vibrant red of Ajvar spread or the smoky heat of paprika spice, we have the perfect pepper to add a kick to your dishes.

Tomatoes

A common and versatile vegetable, tomatoes are used in salads, sauces, and stews. We source vine-ripened tomatoes bursting with flavor and freshness. Serbian tomatoes are the heart of many beloved dishes, from the classic "Srpska Salata" (Serbian Salad) to the rich and flavorful "Punjene Paprike" (Stuffed Peppers).

Potatoes

A widely grown root vegetable, potatoes are a staple in many Serbian dishes. We offer a variety of potato types, each perfect for different culinary applications. From the fluffy baking potatoes to the waxy varieties ideal for potato salad, Serbian potatoes are a versatile ingredient for any meal.
